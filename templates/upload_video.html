Directions

We want to know why you support Open Source Ecology so we're inviting you to create a short 1-2 minute video to tell your friends and everyone else about our work.

There are two ways to create videos:

1. Record via Webcam

Using a webcam and a Youtube account, you can create a video right now on this page. You must have webcam and a Youtube account to record on this page. The video you create will be uploaded to your personal Youtube account as a public video and added to the True Fans video database.

Before recording, you should think about what you are going to say.

2. Manually Link from Existing Video

If you already have a video uploaded to Youtube, you can add it to this page by manually inputting the url.

The privacy on the video must be set to public or unlisted. Please do not set the video to private because no one else will be able to view it.
